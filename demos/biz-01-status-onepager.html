How this was made: the brief, how to reproduce it, and an honesty note

The brief

Here are my raw status notes for this week: [paste your notes].

Create a complete single-file HTML status one-pager for the project:
overall RAG status with a one-line reason, a milestone timeline showing
done / in progress / at risk, budget spent vs plan as a simple bar,
top 3 risks with owners and mitigations, what changed since last week,
and next week's focus. Use only inline CSS and SVG. No external
libraries, no images. It must print cleanly on one A4 page.

How to reproduce

Paste the brief into any capable LLM: GPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok, DeepSeek, or the assistant your company provides. Iterate a few rounds on layout and content until it reads well. Save the final answer as a .html file and open it in any browser. Expect similar output, not identical: every model has its own taste, and that is fine.

Honesty note
